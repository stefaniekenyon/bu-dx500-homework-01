Orientation To Dos
Academics & Advising
- Academic Advising: Contact your assigned faculty advisor to plan and get clearance to register for classes.
- Course Registration: Register for your required and elective courses online through the student registration system.
- Complete Online Orientation: Work through your university's general (and department-specific) online orientation modules.
Campus Life & Financials
- Student ID Photo: Upload a photo online for your campus identification card.
- Financial Aid & Billing: View your tuition statement, set up payment plans, and complete any required financial onboarding.
